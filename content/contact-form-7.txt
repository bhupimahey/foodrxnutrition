Food Rx Contact Form
====================

Import in WordPress: Contact → Add New → switch to "Text" tab → paste below.

Form markup:
------------

<label> First Name (required)
    [text* first-name autocomplete:name] </label>

<label> Last Name (required)
    [text* last-name autocomplete:name] </label>

<label> Email (required)
    [email* your-email autocomplete:email] </label>

<label> Message (required)
    [textarea* your-message] </label>

[acceptance acceptance-terms] I agree to the Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy [/acceptance]

[submit "Send Message"]

Mail tab:
---------
To: your-email@example.com
Subject: Food Rx — New contact form message

From: [your-email]
Reply-To: [your-email]

Message body:
From: [first-name] [last-name]
Email: [your-email]

Message:
[your-message]

---
After creating the form:
1. Note the form ID (shown in the shortcode, e.g. id="123")
2. Optional: run in WP admin or add to wp-config:
   update_option('foodrx_cf7_form_id', 123);
3. Or assign the Food Rx — Contact page template — it auto-finds a form titled "Food Rx Contact"

Messages tab:
-------------
Validation errors acceptance: Please agree to the Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy before submitting.
